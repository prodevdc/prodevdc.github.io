Bone Bot
Bone Bot is my most recent bot. It is a bot only for this server, and you can't let it join your server. list of commands:

For everyone

  • 1. b!help [command]: Shows a general help message or gives specific help for a command.
  • 2. b!profile [member] or b!profile <set / get> <what> [new <what> or member]: Shows someones profile, or edit your profile.
  • 3. b!ping: ping the bot
  • 4. b!ipinfo <ip>: Shows information about an IP
  • 5. b!website: Returns URL for website
  • 6. b!self-role <join / leave> <role name (no @role)>: Give yourself a role
  • 7. b!prefix: Get prefix of bot, don't use b!prefix, but [mention]prefix

Keep in mind argument [ member ] is always [ @member ]!
